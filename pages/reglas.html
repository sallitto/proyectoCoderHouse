  1. Discrimination (whether it be racism, homophobia, etc...) is not tolerated and will be severely punished.
  2. Do not insult or threaten a player's family, friends, pets, etc... Keep it within the boundaries of the virtual game.
  3. Doxing is not tolerated (publishing private real-life information about a player without their permission, such as Facebook, real name, etc...)
  4. Do not repeatedly harass a player, verbally or with in-game actions.

  1. Do not repeatedly grief town allies, such as attacking them, preventing them from hunting, etc...
  2. Using characters with the main purpose of helping opposite town characters is not allowed. eg. helping Elvines to kill Aresdens using an Aresden character, or using an Elvine character to benefit an Aresden character(s).
  3. Do not intentionally let yourself be killed by enemy players without any effort.
  4. Player-agreed duels shall involve both players doing their best to defeat the other. Avoid repeatedly staging duels with the same person.
  5. Using an enemy character to freely and repeatedly give kills to your character or a friend's character will not be tolerated and will result in a large portion of all your Enemy Kills being removed.

  1. Sharing your account or items is not encouraged. The security of your account and its contents is your responsibility.

  1. Buying, selling, or trading in-game goods for anything other than in-game goods or services is not allowed, such as buying or selling items for real money.
  2. Trading accounts is not allowed. Accounts belong to the person that created them, and the e-mail address and/or security questions will be used to verify ownership if required.

  1. The use of third-party programs which modify game behaviour or give an unfair advantage is not allowed. This includes (but is not limited to) modifying sprites, sounds, macros or speed hacking
  2. You may not take advantage of bugs which create unfair advantages or other benefits for yourself or other players. Reporting such exploits must be done privately through a ticket, for which you will be rewarded.
  3. Simultaneously playing on multiple computers or multiple instances of the game is not allowed. Examples include farming crops on one character while hunting on another, or using one character to tank or buff another character of yours. Using another instance of the game to chat or trade is acceptable.
  4. Farming or killing monsters while being away from keyboard (through macros or otherwise) is not allowed.

  1. No spamming. No advertising. No using channels outside their intended uses (non-trades in % trade channel, or trades in global), etc...
